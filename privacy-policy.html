Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Sun-i is designed with privacy at its core. Your documents never leave your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or scanned documents to external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Sun-i does not collect:

  • Your scanned documents or images
  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers for advertising

Local Data Storage

All data processed by Sun-i is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Scanned Documents: Stored locally in your device's secure storage
  • Analysis Reports: Generated and stored locally on your device
  • App Preferences: Language settings and theme preferences are stored locally

You have full control over your data. You can delete any document or all app data at any time through the app or your device settings.

AI Document Analysis

Sun-i uses AI technology to analyze your documents and provide insights:

  • Document analysis is performed to help you understand your documents better
  • AI analysis may involve sending document data to secure AI services for processing
  • We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmission
  • No document content is permanently stored on external servers

Camera and Photo Library Access

Sun-i requires access to:

  • Camera: To scan documents using your device's camera
  • Photo Library: To import existing photos for scanning and to save scanned documents

These permissions are used solely for the app's document scanning functionality. We do not access your photos or camera for any other purpose.

No Account Required

Sun-i does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. You can start scanning documents immediately after installation.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • All local data is protected by iOS's built-in security features
  • Documents are stored using iOS Data Protection
  • No cloud backup of sensitive document data

Children's Privacy

Sun-i is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
