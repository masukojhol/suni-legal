Overview

Sun-i is a document scanning app with AI-powered analysis. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Sun-i is currently in Beta. Features and data practices may change as we improve the app.

Information We Collect

1. Document Data (AI Analysis)

Important: When you use the AI analysis feature, your document images and text are sent to Google's Gemini AI service for processing.
  • Document images are transmitted securely to Gemini AI
  • Analysis is performed by Google's AI service
  • Google may process this data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not permanently store your documents on our servers

2. Analytics Data (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used:

Data Type Purpose
App usage events Improve features
Screen views Understand user flow
Device type Optimize performance
App version Track updates
Crash reports Fix bugs

3. Local Data

The following data is stored only on your device:

  • Scanned document images
  • AI analysis results/reports
  • App preferences (language, theme)

Third-Party Services

Sun-i uses the following third-party services:

Google Gemini AI

  • Used for document analysis
  • Documents are sent to Google's servers for processing
  • Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Firebase (Google)

  • Analytics: Usage tracking and crash reporting
  • Remote Config: App configuration management
  • Subject to Firebase Privacy Policy

How We Use Your Data

  • Document Analysis: To provide AI-powered insights about your documents
  • Analytics: To improve app performance and user experience
  • Remote Config: To manage app features and settings
  • Crash Reports: To identify and fix bugs

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase according to their policies (typically 14 months)
  • AI Processing: Document data is processed by Gemini AI but not permanently stored by us

Camera and Photo Library

Sun-i requires access to:

  • Camera: To scan documents
  • Photo Library: To import and save documents

These permissions are used solely for document scanning functionality.

No Account Required

Sun-i does not require you to create an account. You can use the app without providing personal information like email or name.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete Local Data: Remove documents and reports within the app
  • Uninstall: Remove all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of Analytics: Disable analytics through iOS Settings > Privacy
  • Request Information: Contact us to know what data we have

Data Security

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is protected by iOS security features
  • We do not sell your data to third parties

Children's Privacy

Sun-i is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
